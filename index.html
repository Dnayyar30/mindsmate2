Who are we?

Mind + Mate = A companion for all your intrusive thoughts. A healthy mind leads to a healthy soul, and this mate will be there for you when your other mates aren't. At mindmate we provide support, guidance and curated resources to bridhe the gap in mental health accessibility. MindMate doesn't classify mental well-being as a requirement for just any one age group, and ensures inclusivity from children to elderly. The website provides a general view for quick assurance, but the app is designed to provide solutions personalized to your needs, because every individual mind is unique!.

At MindMate, we care.
